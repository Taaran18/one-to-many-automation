One-to-Many Automations ("we", "our", or "us") operates the platform available at https://one-to-many-automation.vercel.app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

By using our platform, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information
When you register or sign in with Google, we collect:
- Full name
- Email address
- Profile picture (Google login only)
- Company name and type
- Phone number

### 2.2 Google Account Access
When you connect your Gmail account to send emails through our platform, we request access to:
- Your Gmail account to send emails on your behalf (`https://mail.google.com/`)
- Your basic profile information (name, email address)

We do **not** read, store, or share the contents of your Gmail inbox. We only use Gmail access to send emails you explicitly compose or schedule through our platform.

### 2.3 Usage Data
We may collect information about how you use the platform, including:
- Campaign activity and message logs
- Lead and contact data you upload
- Templates you create

### 2.4 Technical Data
- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Device information
- Access timestamps

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide and operate the One-to-Many Automations platform
- Send WhatsApp and email messages on your behalf
- Authenticate your identity and maintain your session
- Improve and develop new features
- Respond to support requests

We do **not** sell your personal information to third parties.

## 4. Google API Data

Our use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the [Google API Services User Data Policy](https://developers.google.com/terms/api-services-user-data-policy), including the Limited Use requirements.

Specifically:
- We only use Gmail access to send emails you initiate through our platform
- We do not transfer Gmail data to third parties
- We do not use Gmail data for advertising purposes
- We do not allow humans to read your Gmail data

## 5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in Supabase (PostgreSQL), hosted on AWS infrastructure. We implement industry-standard security measures including:
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
- Encrypted storage of sensitive credentials
- JWT-based authentication with expiry
